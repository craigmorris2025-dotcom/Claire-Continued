# v16.11-v16.20 Online Operations Endgame Plan

Claire's modern industry-standard online layer should be:

- async-capable where needed
- timeout controlled
- rate-limit aware
- allowlist governed
- source-rights aware
- audit logged
- dependency audited
- replay validated
- operator reviewed
- quarantine capable

This prepares Claire for its endgame direction without letting unverified internet data corrupt deterministic runtime proof.
