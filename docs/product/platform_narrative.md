# Platform Narrative

Verified date: 2026-05-24

Claire is a sovereign intelligence runtime for finding, governing, routing, and packaging high-value opportunities.

The core product is not a chat interface. It is a governed route engine:

- signals enter through controlled source and evidence surfaces
- ACS2 maps trigger, score, route, sequence, output, and failure state
- the center route contract selects portfolio, breakthrough, tech design-build, acquisition, or recursive memory behavior
- the dashboard renders backend truth instead of inventing product state
- every major control is tied to an endpoint, owner, test, gate, and runtime behavior

The shortest description:

Claire turns weak signals into governed strategic, technical, portfolio, and acquisition outputs with proof-bound routing.

The buyer-facing description:

Claire helps strategy, innovation, product, and corporate development teams detect non-obvious shifts, classify what route they belong on, and produce review-ready artifacts without losing source lineage or governance control.

Claire is a FastAPI-backed intelligence operating layer with lifecycle route contracts, ACS2 trigger-score-route maps, governed evidence, endpoint reconciliation, proof-chain validation, standards-control mapping, and design/CAD contract surfaces.
