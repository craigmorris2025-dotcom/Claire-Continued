# Proteus Identity

Verified date: 2026-05-24

Recommended platform name:

Proteus

Why it fits:

- adaptive intelligence without implying randomness
- route-shifting is central to the product
- strategic, technical, portfolio, acquisition, and memory modes can all live under one identity
- the name suggests transformation, but the system remains contract-driven and governed

Positioning line:

Proteus is the governed intelligence routing layer for turning weak signals into strategic, technical, and acquisition-ready outputs.

Naming rule:

Use `Claire` for the local/runtime intelligence persona and `Proteus` for the product/platform identity unless a later brand decision changes this split.
